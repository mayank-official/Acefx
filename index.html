Who We Are

AceFX — Where Craft Meets Cinema

AceFX is a premium post-production studio based in Mumbai, India. We specialise in crafting cinematic content across film, brand advertising, web series, and live events — delivering precision editing, professional colour grading, and immersive sound design.

Founded by a team of passionate filmmakers and editors, our mission is simple — to elevate every frame and help creators tell stories that resonate globally.
